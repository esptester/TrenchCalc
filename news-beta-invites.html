Beta Invites

Published Aug 9, 2025 · 2–4 min read · Category: Community

We’re opening a small beta program to give early access to new EngSiteTools features before they’re publicly released. This is your chance to try upcoming tools, shape their direction, and help us refine them for the real world.

What You’ll Get

  • Early access to unreleased tools and features.
  • Direct feedback channel to our development team.
  • Recognition as a beta contributor in release notes.

Who Can Join

We’re looking for active field engineers, site managers, and contractors who regularly work on excavation and earthworks projects. Your feedback will directly influence how we improve our products.

How to Apply

Send us an email with your role, company, and typical project types. We’ll review applications and invite a small group for the first round.
